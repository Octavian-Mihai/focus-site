## Overview

FocusAccount is a timeline-based productivity app built around one idea: **objective self-accountability**.

Most focus apps just run a timer. FocusAccount lets you plan your entire day on a visual timeline, track your actual work sessions, and then compare the two — so you can see exactly where your discipline held up and where it didn't.

---

## Features

- **24-hour timeline** — Plan your day by dragging blocks directly onto a vertical timeline. Blocks snap to 5-minute intervals and support four types: Focus, Break, Lunch, and Other.
- **Focus timer** — System-time-based timer that stays accurate even when the tab is backgrounded. Supports start, pause, resume, and stop.
- **Planned vs. actual overlay** — Completed sessions appear as dashed overlays on the timeline so you can visually compare intent vs. reality.
- **Discipline score** — A 0–100 daily score based on focus completion rate, schedule adherence, and interruption count.
- **Immersive focus mode** — Fullscreen timer with an animated background. No distractions.
- **Accountability dashboard** — Real-time stats including planned time, actual time, efficiency %, session count, and interruptions.
- **90-day heatmap** — GitHub-style calendar showing daily discipline scores over the past 90 days.
- **Persistent storage** — All data saved to `localStorage`. No account or backend required.
